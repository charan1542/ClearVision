OCULINGUA

OcuLingua is an NLP-based eye disease detection model that analyzes medical text, such as patient symptoms, clinical notes, or reports. It uses natural language processing techniques to identify keywords, patterns, and contextual clues related to eye conditions. OcuLingua helps healthcare professionals interpret unstructured text data, supporting faster and more informed decision-making in eye care.

STEPS TO USE OUR OCUSCAN

STEP 1 : SELECT SYMPTOMS

Select relatable symptoms in the poolof options.

STEP 2 : AUTOMATED ANALYSIS

OcuLingua uses NLP techniques to understand and detect signs of eye diseases.

STEP 3 : VIEW RESULTS

Get a detailed report on the predicted disease based on the symptoms you selected.

STEP 4 : CONSULT A SPECIALIST

Use the insights to seek professional medical advice for further evaluation.
