OCUSCAN

OcuScan is an advanced eye disease detection model that utilizes Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) and YOLO (You Only Look Once) for precise and real-time analysis of eye images. CNNs extract detailed features from retinal and ocular images, while YOLO enables fast and accurate localization of disease-affected regions. By combining these technologies, OcuScan ensures efficient early diagnosis, supporting healthcare professionals in providing timely and effective eye care.

STEPS TO USE OUR OCUSCAN

STEP 1 : UPLOAD IMAGE

Select and Upload a Clear eye or Fundus image for analysis.

STEP 2 : AUTOMATED ANALYSIS

OcuScan uses CNN and YOLO models to detect potential eye diseases.

STEP 3 : VIEW RESULTS

Get a detailed report on detected conditions with confidence scores.

STEP 4 : CONSULT A SPECIALIST

Use the insights to seek professional medical advice for further evaluation.
