Retinal Diseases

Retinal Detachment

Description:

Separation of the retina from the underlying tissue

Symptoms:

Sudden vision loss, Floaters, Flashes of light, Side vision loss

Learn More

Diabetic Retinopathy

Description:

Damage to the retinal blood vessels due to diabetes

Symptoms:

Blurred vision, Dark spots, Vision loss, Fluctuating vision, Floaters

Learn More

Macular Degeneration

Description:

Degeneration of the macula, affecting central vision

Symptoms:

Loss of central vision, Difficulty reading

Learn More

Retinitis Pigmentosa

Description:

Genetic disorder causing progressive vision loss

Symptoms:

Night blindness, Tunnel vision, Progressive vision loss, Loss of side vision, Light sensitivity, Loss of color visison

Learn More

Macular Edema

Description:

Swelling in the macula due to fluid leakage

Symptoms:

Blurred or distorted vision, Color changes

Learn More

Central Serous Retinopathy

Description:

Fluid accumulation under the retina causing vision distortion

Symptoms:

Blurred central vision, Dark spot in vision, Darker or dim vision

Learn More

Hypertensive Retinopathy

Description:

Retinal damage due to high blood pressure

Symptoms:

Blurred vision, Narrowed vision field

Learn More

Retinal Vein Occlusion

Description:

Blockage of veins draining blood from the retina, leading to swelling and vision loss

Symptoms:

Sudden vision loss, Floaters, Blurred vision, Pain

Learn More

Eyelid Diseases

Blepharitis

Description:

Inflammation of the eyelid margins due to bacteria or skin conditions

Symptoms:

Red, Swollen eyelids, Crusted eyelashes, Irritation, Watery eyes, Foreign body sensation, More frequent blinking

Learn More

Chalazion

Description:

Blocked meibomian gland leading to a painless eyelid lump

Symptoms:

Painless eyelid lump, Swelling, Mild irritation

Learn More

Hordeolum (Stye)

Description:

Infected oil gland in the eyelid causing a painful red bump

Symptoms:

Painful, Red bump on eyelid, Swelling, Excessive tearing

Learn More

Ptosis

Description:

Drooping of the upper eyelid, often due to muscle weakness

Symptoms:

Drooping eyelid, Vision obstruction

Learn More

Entropion

Description:

Inward turning of the eyelid, causing eyelashes to irritate the eye

Symptoms:

Eye irritation, Tearing, Redness, Eye pain, Crusting

Learn More

Ectropion

Description:

Outward turning of the eyelid, leading to dryness and irritation

Symptoms:

Dry eyes, Irritation, Redness, Foreign body sensation, Mucus discharge

Learn More

Lagophthalmos

Description:

Inability to fully close the eyelids, leading to dry eyes

Symptoms:

Dry eyes, Excessive tearing, Irritation, Blurred visison, Redness

Learn More

Blepharospasm

Description:

Uncontrolled eyelid twitching or spasms

Symptoms:

Frequent, Uncontrolled blinking or spasms

Learn More

Conjuctiva Diseases

Viral Conjunctivitis

Description:

Highly contagious viral infection of the eye causing redness and watery discharge

Symptoms:

Redness, Mild itching, Watery discharge, Mild irritation, Clear and thin discharge, Starts in one eye and spread to other, Other viral symptoms

Learn More

Bacterial Conjunctivitis

Description:

Bacterial infection of the conjunctiva leading to thick discharge and redness

Symptoms:

Redness, Itching, Thick discharge, Irritation, Yellow or greenish discharge, Morning crusting

Learn More

Allergic Conjunctivitis

Description:

Allergic reaction causing eye redness, itching, and tearing

Symptoms:

Redness, Itching, Swelling, Excessive tearing, Other allergy related symptoms, Affects both eyes simultaneously

Learn More

Cornea Diseases

Keratitis

Description:

Inflammation of the cornea due to infection, trauma, or dryness

Symptoms:

Redness, Pain, Excessive tearing, Blurred vision, Sensitivity to light, Foreign body sensation

Learn More

Keratoconus

Description:

Progressive thinning and bulging of the cornea leading to vision distortion

Symptoms:

Blurred vision, Distorted vision, Light sensitivity, Difficulty seeing at night, Frequent prescription changes, Sudden worsening or clouding of vision

Learn More

Corneal Ulcer

Description:

Open sore on the cornea caused by infection, injury, or contact lens overuse

Symptoms:

Severe eye pain, Redness, Blurred vision, White or gray spot on cornea, Watery discharge, Sensitivity to light

Learn More

Fuchs' Dystrophy

Description:

Slowly progressing corneal disorder affecting the endothelium, leading to swelling

Symptoms:

Morning blurred vision, Glare, Halos around lights, Eye pain, Corneal swelling, Poor night vision

Learn More

Corneal Dystrophy

Description:

Group of genetic disorders causing corneal clouding and vision impairment

Symptoms:

Blurred vision, Hazy vision, Eye pain, Sensitivity to light, Foreign body sensation

Learn More

Corneal Erosion

Description:

Recurrent corneal epithelial defects causing pain and light sensitivity

Symptoms:

Sudden eye pain (morning), Excessive tearing, Blurry vision, Light sensitivity

Learn More

Glaucoma

Primary Open-Angle Glaucoma

Description:

Most common form, slow drainage of aqueous humor leads to increased eye pressure

Symptoms:

Gradual vision loss, Blind spots, Tunnel vision in later stages

Learn More

Angle-Closure Glaucoma

Description:

Blocked drainage angle causes sudden rise in eye pressure, medical emergency

Symptoms:

Severe eye pain, Headache, Nausea, Blurred vision, Halos around lights

Learn More

Vitreous Diseases

Vitreous Detachment

Description:

Separation of the vitreous gel from the retina, common with aging

Symptoms:

Floaters, Flashes of light, Blurry vision

Learn More

Vitreous Hemorrhage

Description:

Bleeding into the vitreous humor due to trauma, diabetes, or retinal conditions

Symptoms:

Sudden vision loss, Floaters, Red tint in vision

Learn More

Asteroid Hyalosis

Description:

Benign condition with calcium and lipid deposits in the vitreous

Symptoms:

Floaters, Sparkling particles in vision

Learn More

Persistent Fetal Vasculature

Description:

Failure of fetal blood vessels to regress properly, affecting vision

Symptoms:

Leukocoria (white pupil), Vision impairment

Learn More

Vitreomacular Traction Syndrome

Description:

Abnormal adhesion between vitreous and macula, leading to vision distortion

Symptoms:

Distorted central vision, Blurred vision

Learn More

Endophthalmitis

Description:

Severe eye infection affecting the vitreous, can cause blindness

Symptoms:

Severe eye pain, Redness, Vision loss, Pus in eye

Learn More

Lens Diseases

Cataract

Description:

Clouding of the lens, common in aging

Symptoms:

Blurred vision, Glare sensitivity, Faded colors, Light sensitivity, Dim vision, Double vision, Poor night vision

Learn More

Presbyopia

Description:

Age-related loss of near vision due to lens stiffening

Symptoms:

Difficulty reading small text, Eye strain

Learn More

Aphakia

Description:

Absence of the lens (congenital or post-surgery)

Symptoms:

Blurred vision, Difficulty focusing, Problems seeing things that are close and far away, Problems seeing the brightness of colors, Faded colors

Learn More

Ectopia Lentis

Description:

Displacement of the lens due to genetic conditions (Marfan Syndrome, Homocystinuria)

Symptoms:

Double vision, Myopia, Lens misalignment, Blurred vision, Redness

Learn More

Nuclear Sclerosis

Description:

Hardening of the central part of the lens (early-stage cataract)

Symptoms:

Gradual decline in vision, Glare sensitivity, Light sensitivity, Blurry vision, Difficulty seeing in bright light

Learn More
