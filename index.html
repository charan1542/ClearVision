OcuScan

OcuScan is a simple and effective eye disease detection model that helps identify eye conditions by analyzing images. It is designed to assist healthcare professionals in detecting issues early. OcuScan aims to improve eye care through quick and reliable results.

OcuLingua

NLP MAKES IT

CONVINENT

OcuLingua is an NLP-based model designed to detect eye diseases by analyzing textual medical data. It helps healthcare professionals interpret reports, symptoms, and descriptions for accurate diagnosis. OcuLingua aims to enhance eye care through efficient and reliable text analysis.
